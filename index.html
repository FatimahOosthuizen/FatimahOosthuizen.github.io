How we roll at Pargo

> How we work

We have a flexible work policy. We see the value of our team being the office and having the opportunity to connect and work together face to face. We also see how having the opportunity and flexibility to work from home can allow you to be more productive. Our work policy is two days in the office per week (on the same days as the rest of your team) and then the other three days are optional in the office or at home depending on your preference. One Friday a month we all come together as a team in the office for our monthly review.

> How we communicate

At Pargo we use Slack to communicate internally. A Slack Channel Should be the central place where all the conversations about a particular channel happen to ensure that we working fast at high quality while being transparent. If it isn't confidential client or employee data, put in on the channel. Always assume good intent and give the feedback to the thing, not the person.

> Team meetings

We meet as a team once every Thursday from 10am for up to an hour to catch up on everything that has been happening. Each team meets differently depending on the team but most teams have weekly planning and retros and daily stand ups. Once a month we have an In Office team meeting where all team members are expected to be in office.
